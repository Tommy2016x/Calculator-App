How is this special?

At first glance this may seem like any basic Calculator App, well it is... but not quite. For example this calculator behaves just like a real calculator should. I have searched around for examples of other Javascript calculators but most only do one operation at a time and display the text in an awkward way, such as (5+9+3+5) and often times you cannot re-use the operation symbols more than once. Look around the web at other examples and you'll see what I mean
